1.1 – Convert 234 (base-10) to binary
To covert we divide the number by two and the remainders will form our binary

234 /2 repeatedly
234 - 117 remainder 0
117 - 58 remainder 1
58 - 29 remainder 0
29- 14 remainder 1
14 -7 remainder 0
7- 3 remainder 1
3 - 1 remainder 1
1- 0 remainder 1

Our binary will be 11101010 base 2 
234 in base 10 = 11101010 base 2
